WizeBit Vision?

We are dedicated to making your home smarter by allowing you to control every Internet of Things (IoT) device without exposing your data to third parties. By using a localized version of the WizeBit blockchain, you keep all data local while bringing an entirely new level automation and control to your house via devices like the WizeBox Diamond and the WizeBox Mini. Moreover, each box mines cryptocurrency for you - generating revenue for you without any effort from you. This is the smarter IoT home. This is Wize.

Earn

Each WizeBox mines approximately 3-21 of the WIZE cryptocurrency per month. WIZE coins will be exchangeable on centralized and decentralized cryptocurrency exchanges. They will also be the main currency for services within the Wize infrastructure (thus increasing their value as our network grows).

Control

With WizeBox, we focus on function, i.e. how seamlessly and intuitively you can control every IoT device in your home. To that end, we are developing integrations and partnerships with more and more IoT devices every day.

Protect

None of the big players in the smart hub space protect your data the way WizeBox does. These companies built their businesses on data. Do you want them to profit from yours? With WizeBox, your data stays with you, in your home. It’s the simple, fair, and safe way to control your home’s IoT devices.WizeBox runs on a local version of the Wize blockchain - local to your home. So your data never leaves your personal network - it’s protected against hacking and other malicious interactions.

Luna is open source | Built by Community

Luna lives on the open-source WizeBit ecosystem, which uses WIZE Tokens to incentivize developers to create apps and community members to suggest features they want Luna to acquire or improve. Ideas are either upvoted or downvoted to form a Community Consensus, where only the best succeed. The idea’s creators gets rewarded with WIZE Tokens and Luna steps up her game.

A.I. Assistant

Luna is the personal assistant current virtual assistants aspire to be: she uses AI machine learning to get to know what you mean and intend by your requests, your habits, preferences, likes, and dislikes.

Cryptographic

Powerfull

Anonymous User Profile

Problem

As a society, we used to value privacy: just like every window has curtains, we’ve kept our personal conversations private and information about our habits to ourselves (and maybe a few of our closest friends). Yet, these days, we post the most private details on Facebook for the world to see. At least we do that consciously. But what about troves of our very personal data collected by the various devices we use: from credit cards to cars. Smart home devices are no exception. Even more so, these devices have access to information about your every habit at home. Like to get up in the middle of the night to visit the bathroom (or kitchen, no judgement here)? Do you want advertisers to know about this?

Solution

We here at WizeBit find this constant collecting and selling of your data disturbing. We believe in a world where people fully collect they personal data and have their privacy 100% protected. IoT and privacy are not mutually exclusive. Neither are the blockchain and privacy. Everything we create at WizeBit - from devices to services - is built around our unswerving conviction that only you have the right to your data and that that your total privacy should always be in your hands. Agree? Let’s build a smarter IoT home together - one that respects your privacy.

FAQ

How is WizeBit different from Amazon Alexa, or Google Home?

WizeBit keeps your data local and secure and does not spy on you through a microphone or camera. Also, WizeBit does not sell your data to third parties.

How does WizeBit store your data?

All of the data that WizeBit collects, it encrypts and stores on a blockchain. Only people with the appropriate keys (you) will be able to access this data.

Why was WizeBit created?

WizeBit was created to provide an AI-based personal assistant (Luna) that does not invade your privacy and exploit user data to sell to third parties.

Can anybody develop code for consensus approval from the WizeBit community?

Yes, anyone who is a member of the WizeBit community can suggest code to improve the WizeBox. However, suggesting ideas does not mean that they will be automatically approved.

What reward do developers get for suggesting an idea that receives consensus?

They receive a certain amount of WIZE tokens.

How many WIZE tokens will ever be in existence?

The total amount of WIZE tokens that will ever exist is 500 million.

How does WIzeBit securely manage your IOT devices?

The WizeBox monitors the IOT devices in your home in real time and securely stores the history of the monitoring data. Blockchain technology is used for this data storage. End-to-end secure protocols are used for these transactions.

Can anyone use WizeBox?

Yes, anyone with an internet connection can use WizeBox.

Am I the only person who will be able to access my WizeBox?

Yes, because of voice recognition biometrics, only your unique voice will be able to access WizeBit. This heightens security.

Can WIzeBit detect fires and sound the alarm?

Yes, WizeBox can connect to your smoke detectors and help sound the alarm if there is a fire.

Can WizeBox mine any other cryptocurrencies?

The WizeBox 1.0 will only mine WizeCoins.

How many Wize Tokens is locked for miners?

WizeBit Miners can mine Wize Token for 12 years. (100M token is locked for miners)
